Project 1: Call 4 Short (C4S)

C4S is an AI-based text summarizer for earnings calls transcripts.
My objective is to provide financial analysts with a user-friendly chatbot that helps in quickly extracting valuable metrics and opinions expressed in an earnings calls transcript.
